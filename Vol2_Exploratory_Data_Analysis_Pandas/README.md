**📊 Volume Overview:**
---
***This Volume contains a data-driven market performance analysis comparing two key FMCG products: DOVE NUTRITIVE and SUNSILK EXPERT. The volume utilizes Python especially Pandas to clean, aggregate sales data, aiming to extract actionable business insights regarding market penetration, profitability, and sales dynamics.***
---
**📁 Repository Structure & Methodology:**
---
  - **0. Methodology and Setup Section:**

- ***Description:*** This section outlines the initial data ingestion and preparation phase. It covers importing the raw sales dataset, converting data types (e.g., datetime formatting), and filtering the records to isolate the target products.

- ***Purpose:*** To ensure data accuracy and create a clean, focused foundation for all subsequent financial and commercial analyses.
---
  - **1. KPIs Analysis Section:**

- ***Description:*** This notebook focuses on calculating the Achievement Rate percentage by comparing the actual generated revenue against the predefined sales targets.

- ***Purpose:*** To quickly evaluate which product is successfully meeting its business goals and driving commercial success.
---
  - **2. Variance Analysis Section:**

- ***Description:*** This analysis measures the absolute financial difference (Variance) between actual sales and target expectations, visualizing positive gains and negative shortfalls.

- ***Purpose:*** To quantify exact profit margins or deficits, highlighting specific areas that require immediate strategic intervention or marketing support.
---
  - **3. Volume vs Value Analysis Section:**

-  ***Description:*** A comparative study between the physical volume of goods moved (Units Sold) and the financial value generated (Revenue), including the calculation of the Average Revenue Per Unit.

- ***Purpose:*** To assess the effectiveness of current pricing strategies and determine if a high sales volume is genuinely translating into high profitability.
---
  - **4. Trend Analysis Section:**
-  ***Description:*** This notebook tracks daily sales performance using time-series line charts to visualize revenue fluctuations over the given period.

-  ***Purpose:*** To identify market trends, peak sales days, and consumer purchasing patterns, providing a data-backed basis for future inventory management and promotional campaigns.
